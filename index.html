FAQ

Can a free page look legitimate?

Yes. Design quality matters more than platform cost at this stage. A clean hero section, strong messaging, custom domain, and clear calls to action already put you ahead of most early concepts.

What works for free and what does not?

Free works well for brand pages, launch teasers, contact buttons, product storytelling, image galleries, and simple lead capture. Full ecommerce checkout, inventory, and advanced CRM are better later on a paid platform.

Can this be updated without coding much?

Yes. You can keep editing the text, links, emails, and images in a single file. Once you get comfortable, it becomes a very fast way to keep the site fresh without monthly software cost.
