Food safety & shelf stability

Shelf-stable by USDA-regulated retort processing

Meatyer is designed to be produced as a thermally processed, commercially sterile (TPCS) product: real wood smoked meat is sealed in a retort pouch and heat-processed using a validated scheduled process. The result is a safe, stable product that can live on the shelf and hit meals fast when you need it.

After opening, treat like cooked meat and refrigerate.

Hermetically sealed pouch

Sealed packaging is built to protect the contents during and after thermal processing.

Scheduled thermal process

A processing authority establishes time/temperature targets to achieve safety and stability.

HACCP + records

USDA-inspected production uses HACCP controls, sanitation SOPs, and recordkeeping to verify the process.
